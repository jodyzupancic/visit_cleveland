Visit the Cudell/West Boulevard/Edgewater Neighborhoods of Cleveland!

Enjoy amazing food, historic buildings, the lakefront, and an indoor mountain bike park in this often overlooked gem on Cleveland's West Side.

Enjoy indoor mountain biking

Ray's Indoor Mountain Bike Park is the world's first and largest multi-terrain indoor bike park.

Eat authentic Salvadoran cuisine

Eat the best pupusas in Cleveland at Pupuseria La Bendicion.

Experience Lake Erie

Walk, bike, swim, jetski, or picnic at scenic Edgewater Park.

Check out historic West Tech High School

Built in 1912, Cleveland's first vocational school is an historic landmark.
